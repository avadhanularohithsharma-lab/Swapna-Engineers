Who We Are
About Us

Founded in 2010 by Mrs. Swapna Srinivas & Mr. Srinivas, Swapna Engineers has grown from a local plumbing service into one of India's recognised MEP contracting organisations — trusted by Tier-1 developers across multiple states.

Our company stands out for its end-to-end service capability: from a single tap replacement to full-scale township installations covering hundreds of apartments. We bring the same rigour and professionalism to every project, regardless of its size.

With a strong presence across Telangana, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Andhra Pradesh, we are equipped to mobilise quickly and deliver with quality — every time.

What We Do
Core Services
🔧
Plumbing Works
Comprehensive internal & external plumbing for residential towers, commercial complexes, hotels, and institutional buildings. Supply, installation, and commissioning from source to fixture.
🔥
Fire Fighting Systems
Design, supply & installation of hydrant systems, sprinkler networks, and fire suppression equipment — compliant with NBC and IS standards. Serving high-rise, commercial, and residential projects.
🎨
Painting Works
Large-scale interior and exterior painting for industrial, commercial, and residential projects. Premium materials with meticulous surface preparation and finish quality.
💧
Waterproofing
Terrace, basement, wet area, and retaining wall waterproofing using tested, premium-grade systems. Preventing leakage and structural degradation for the long term.
